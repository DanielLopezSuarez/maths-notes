    Welcome to Math Mastery

    From Linear Algebra to Differential Equations.

    Welcome to this comprehensive mathematical documentation. This project serves as both a theoretical reference and a practical guide, bridging the gap between pure mathematics and computational applications using Python.

    üìö Course Modules

    We have structured the content into three logical pillars, taking you from data structures (matrices) to the study of change (calculus).

    1. Algebra: The Structure

    Focuses on Linear Algebra, the foundation of modern computing and data science.

    • Matrices: Definitions, operations, and special types.
    • Determinants: Sarrus, Laplace, and geometric interpretation.
    • Inverse Matrices: Cofactors and Gauss-Jordan elimination.
    • Systems of Equations: Solving \(Ax=b\) using Cramer's Rule, Inverses, and Gaussian Elimination.

    2. Geometry: The Space

    Focuses on Vector Analysis and Analytic Geometry in 3D space. * Vectors: Operations, magnitude, and unit vectors. * Vector Products: Dot product (angles), Cross product (normals), and Triple product (volumes). * Analytic Geometry: Defining Lines and Planes in 3D space and analyzing their intersections.

    3. Calculus: The Engine

    The study of continuous change, motion, and accumulation. * Functions & Limits: The rigorous foundation of analysis. * Derivatives: Instantaneous rates of change and optimization. * Integrals: Area, volume, and accumulation (Riemann & Newton-Leibniz). * Differential Equations: Modeling real-world phenomena (growth, decay, motion).

    üêç Python & Calculator

    This project isn't just about formulas; it's about application. Throughout the notes, you will find: * Python Implementations: How to solve math problems using NumPy. * The Calculator: A custom Python script included in this repo to perform matrix operations, vector calculations, and more directly from your terminal.
