# Geometry Formulas

Essential formulas for shapes, areas, and volumes.

## 1. Pythagorean Theorem
Relates the sides of a right-angled triangle.

$$
a^2 + b^2 = c^2
$$

## 2. Area of a Triangle
Basic area formula given base $b$ and height $h$.

$$
A = \frac{1}{2}bh
$$

## 3. Area of a Circle
Area given the radius $r$.

$$
A = \pi r^2
$$

## 4. Circumference of a Circle
The distance around the circle.

$$
C = 2\pi r
$$

## 5. Volume of a Sphere
Volume of a 3D ball.

$$
V = \frac{4}{3}\pi r^3
$$

## 6. Surface Area of a Sphere
The outer area of a 3D ball.

$$
A = 4\pi r^2
$$

## 7. Volume of a Cylinder
Volume given radius $r$ and height $h$.

$$
V = \pi r^2 h
$$

## 8. Law of Sines
Relates sides and angles in any triangle.

$$
\frac{a}{\sin A} = \frac{b}{\sin B} = \frac{c}{\sin C}
$$

## 9. Law of Cosines
Generalization of the Pythagorean theorem for any triangle.

$$
c^2 = a^2 + b^2 - 2ab \cos(C)
$$

## 10. Sum of Interior Angles
For a polygon with $n$ sides.

$$
S = (n - 2) \cdot 180^{\circ}
$$
